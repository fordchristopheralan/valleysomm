## Notes on Personalization

**Always personalize:**
- Winery name (obviously)
- Contact name (find on website or LinkedIn)
- Something specific about their winery (signature wine, unique feature, recent award)
- Reference to any previous interaction

**Research before sending:**
- Check their website for news/events
- Look at recent Instagram posts
- Note any awards or press mentions
- Find the right person (owner, tasting room manager, marketing)

**Best times to send:**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm
- Avoid Mondays (busy catching up)
- Avoid Fridays (already thinking about weekend)
- Never on weekends (wineries are slammed)
