Concise Impact

Brand Voice Guidelines

Conversational

Speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a formal guide. Use "you" and "your" to create personal connection.

Expert-Yet-Relaxed

Share wine knowledge confidently but accessibly. Avoid pretentious jargon—make wine approachable.

Exciting & Inspiring

Build anticipation for adventures. Use vivid language about landscapes, flavors, and discoveries.

Inclusive & Fun

Welcome all experience levels. Celebrate curiosity. Make planning feel like the start of an adventure.

Landscapes & Scenery

Rolling NC hills, vineyard rows at golden hour, misty valley mornings, rustic winery exteriors with modern touches. Natural, warm lighting preferred.

Wine & Tastings

Close-ups of wine in glasses with natural settings, grape clusters on vines, diverse groups enjoying tastings, candid moments of discovery and connection.

Tech Integration

Clean app UI screenshots with chat bubbles, phone mockups in winery settings, subtle digital overlays. Tech should feel seamless, not intrusive.
